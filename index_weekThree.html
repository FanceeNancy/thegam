I love my cat!

His name is Moon.

Moon is a black cat, possibly a Bombay. I adopted him from ACC in March. He came home with a few illnesses and I thought our maladies would line up well. He had a polyp in his ear and esophageal-nasal stenosis. He underwent some surgeries and came out a stronger, happier kitty.

He likes playing with all of his toys, especially those lattice balls with little bells in them. He chases those around the apartment endlessly!

His favorite toys are:

  • Lattice Balls
  • Mr. Bird Wand Toy
  • Springys
  • Senses Ball Track

We have a catio set up outside so he can get a 3D view of birds, but the birds remain safe from his deadly grasp.

Moon has taken well to clicker training, and now wakes me up with a gentle tap on the shoulder. He just celebrated his 1st birthday on October 21st! We celebrated with new toys and a long nap.
