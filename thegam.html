The Gam is a bookclub named for a lesser known definition of Gam. We read classics and classic-adjacent books and then travel to some place relevant to the text.

In 2023, we read Don Quixote and then traveled to Spain! We ate great food and saw a lot of windmills. Our favorite thing about Spain were the siestas. It's always good to rest from 3pm - 5pm.
